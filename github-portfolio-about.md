## Professional Background

I am a life sciences professional specializing in **cell therapy**, working to help cancer patients by engineering immune cells within the human body. I have been working in the United States for over four years, and previously studied at **Tufts University** and the **University of Pennsylvania** for my undergraduate and master's degrees. 

I chose to pursue biology because I wanted to help others through my work and contribute to society in a way that fulfills my sense of purpose. **I am deeply passionate about what I do.**
